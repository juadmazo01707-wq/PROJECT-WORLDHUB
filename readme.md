# KEPLER 22B - Fictional World

An interactive web project that explores the fictional world of the exoplanet Kepler 22B, where three distinct kingdoms inhabit unique conditions of light and darkness.

## Project Description 

Kepler 22B ia a synchronously rotating exoplanet with three distinct regions: 

- **Realm of light**: Permanently iluminated hemisphere.
- **Realm of darkness**: Hemisphere in perpetual shadow.  
- **Middle realm**: Zone of transition and balance.

## Key Features

### Inttuitive Navigation 
- Side menu with expandable sections.
- Internal navigation with anchors.
- Back button on each subpage.

### Responsive Design 
- Adaptable to mobile devices.
- Automatic background video.
- Collapsible sidebar.

### Content
- Detailed descriptions of each realm.
- Information on physical characteristics.
- Unique cultures and societies.
- Fantastic creatures adapted to the environment.

## Los Tres Reinos

### The Three Kingdoms (Sinteryum)
- **Government**: Sun Queen (Elara Voss).
- **Location**: Permanently iluminated hermisphere.
- **Characteristics**: Golden skin, bright eyes, high intellectual capacity.
- **Technology**: Solar energy harnessing, floating cities.
- **Institution**: Counsex-oversees research and administration.
- **Creatures**:
  - **Fotovores**: Flying herbivores with photovoltaic cells.
  - **Lumin serpientes**: Bioluminescent reptiles.
  - **Auror aves**: Migratory birds with refractive feathers.

### Kingdom of Darkness (Tenebrion)
- **Government**: Lunar king (Draven Kael).
- **Location**: Hemisphere in perpetual shadow.
- **Characterístics**: Matte black skin, eyes adapted to darkness.
- **Technology**: Underground engineering, geothermal energy.
- **Society**: Underground cities, ultrasonic communication.
- **Creatures**:
  - **Ecosombras**: Underground mammals with giant ears.
  - **Geotermos**: Colossal insects that extract heat form the core.
  - **Susurro espíritus**: Ethereal creatures that communicate.

### Middle Kingdom (Equinoxis)
- **Government**: Neutral dipomatic system.
- **Location**: Transition zone "The Midnight Strip".
- **Characterístics**: Chaning skin, adaptable eyes.
- **Technology**: Light/Dark regulators, inter-kingdom trade.
- **Function**: Diplomacy, trade, balance between kingdoms.
- **Hybrid Creatures**:
  - **Equinox vipers**: Snakes with adaptive scales.
  - **Twilight voladores**: Migratory trade birds.
  - **Limbofagos**: Guardians of valuable resources.
